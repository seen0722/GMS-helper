# GMS-Helper System Demo Video Script

**Target Audience:** High-level Executives
**Goal:** Demonstrate efficiency and AI capabilities of the GMS-helper system.
**Format:** Screen recording + Voiceover (VO)
**Estimated Duration:** 2 - 2.5 Minutes

## üé¨ Preparation Checklist

## üí° Tips for Recording
1.  **Zoom In:** Ensure text is readable. Zoom to 110% or 125% in the browser if needed.
2.  **Pause:** After clicking a button, wait 1 second before moving the mouse again. This makes editing easier.
3.  **High-Light:** If your recording software allows, add a subtle highlight around the mouse cursor.
